
# Player

- Every (3) turns is a Round.
  Each turn:
          Draw 5 cards to hand.
          Roll for move.  (D6)
 
- Player’s Action:
          Choose to play card(s)
          Buy/Place Buildings
          Decide where to move to
          Combat (if landed in space with enemy)
          Buy cards from the market
 
- Each Round, New Enemy and Trouble card spawn
- Enemy will move toward Center (?)

 
# Goal:
         Play for (X) Rounds. Survive.
         Earn Fame (VP) for score.
         Protect the “Heart of the City” from enemy
         Don’t let the Moral fall too low
